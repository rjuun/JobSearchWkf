## 1. What is the problem or opportunity?

How can I create the process of going every week and checking the target companies for positions in a consequential manner ?

## 2. What would the improvement look like?

Being able to see the list of target companies for a given location and ticking each one of them for a given kind of filter.

In my [Job Hunting - Companies](https://nunonine.sharepoint.com/sites/JobHunting/Lists/Companies/AllItems.aspx) list, we must be more consequent in having the `Job Alert`
column transformed into `Monitoring Method`:
- Job Alert is one Monitoring Method
- Scrapping would be another Monitoring Method
- Manually

And then we would need to have some way to indicate that we "checked" it for the week.

Also, instead of having the alerts coming to our regular email, the idea is to have being redirected to an specific folder.

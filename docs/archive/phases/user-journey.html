JobSearch Camunda · User-Journey Plan

Your inputs are the moat

Generic AI turns a CV + a job ad into a tailored CV — generic, forgetful, prone to invention. This system's power is the rich, human-curated evidence behind it. The plan: make that evidence base — the Career Graph — something a new user can build in minutes and grow forever, with an honest AI co-pilot that drafts while the human decides.

Career Graph as the asset Hybrid onboarding: import → coach AI drafts · you curate Truthfulness as a feature Senior operators first

The moat — why this beats generic AI

Generic AI CV tools

  • Stateless: (CV + JD) → CV, every time from scratch
  • Generic phrasing; no memory of you
  • Optimises impressiveness over truth — invents metrics
  • Nothing compounds; you start over each app

This system

  • A Career Graph: positions → STAR stories → actions, quantified results, skills — every claim cited by ref_code
  • Human judgment gates: you approve every claim (Green/Yellow/Red); honesty is enforced
  • Gets more personal the more it's used
  • A career asset you own and grow

Today that moat is a 12-sheet Excel wall — pre-seeded, un-buildable in-product. Closing that gap, approachably, is the whole plan.

Who we build for first

SO

The Senior Operator

15+ years; exec / finance / transformation / chief-of-staff; multi-country, non-linear career. Applies selectively, time-poor, reputation-sensitive — won't risk a fabricated claim, and already distrusts AI "slop." The owner can dogfood it; truthfulness matters most where stakes are highest.

  • 1"Help me articulate what I've actually done, at the level it deserves."
  • 2"Tell me honestly which roles are worth my scarce time."
  • 3"Produce a truthful, tailored, ATS-safe CV per role in minutes."
  • 4"Build a career asset I own that gets better over time."

Design principles

1
AI drafts, the human curates & enriches — everywhere.

Lowers input cost and keeps it true. One consistent, trust-building pattern across onboarding and tailoring.

2
Start with a win, not a wall.

A minimally-viable graph unlocks value; never block on completeness.

3
Make the moat visible.

Show graph completeness & strength as an asset worth growing.

4
Trust by provenance.

Every claim traces to evidence the user approved. Truthfulness is a feature, not a constraint.

5
Two speeds.

A guided wizard for newcomers; a power path (bulk/table editor + Excel) for operators like the owner.

6
The graph is the product; jobs are transient.

Invert today's lead-centric IA — Profile becomes first-class.

7
Coach, don't just collect.

The STAR/impact interview draws out under-told wins — the highest-value job for senior pros.

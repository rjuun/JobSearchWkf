A verdict before the commit

The door already carries its B6 fit, coverage, and gaps — so the verdict is a pure projection, not a new model. "Test this door →" opens a panel with one honest line, the one or two things you'd strengthen first, and the same flag CTA. It's deterministic and it refuses to oversell: a high fit with thin coverage is called a stretch, never an adjacency.

Adjacency

Strong fit and most must-haves covered — "a door you can walk through."

Stretch

Reachable, but you'd walk in with N gaps to close first — "worth it only if the direction pulls you."

Reach

The evidence isn't there yet — "a direction to grow toward, not a next move."

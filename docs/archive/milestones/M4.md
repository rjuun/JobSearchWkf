## The flow (decisions & time)

1. Paste CV + job ad → **Screen this role** (1 action, no forms).
2. Land in the workspace on a real Role-Fit verdict + the screening watch-out.
3. Curate on demand: Keep the ~6 evidence items the CV needs (each Keep = onto the CV + into the graph) → **Generate** (existing C2→C7 flow).
4. → a downloadable CV. Total ≈ 8 decisions, well under the ≤10 / ≤5-min bar.

## Deliberate simplifications (vs. the full Phase 3 vision)

- **Draft committed up-front as `imported`, not per-Keep of still-draft nodes.** The doc's purest form keeps
  every node "draft" until its Keep, and screens against an in-memory draft graph. Here the import is
  committed as *imported history* (honest provenance), screened, and labelled "based on your imported
  history"; the C2 Keep gate then curates what reaches the **CV**. This reuses the whole existing
  screening/tailoring pipeline and hits the activation bar. A truer per-Keep-of-draft model (with a
  draft/approved flag on evidence rows) is a follow-up.
- **The verdict + curation live in the existing workspace** rather than a bespoke streamed cold-start
  screen; the "epilogue graph-born" moment is the `/profile` graph + the queued coach session.
- **First-win instrumentation** (time-to-first-CV, decisions-before-win) and the **no-job-ad fallback**
  (Path C) are **M5**, as sequenced.

## Testing steps (manual)

1. Sign up a fresh account (or clear the graph) → you land on **/start**.
2. Click **Use a sample** on both boxes → **Screen this role** → you land in the workspace with a verdict and the "imported history" banner.
3. In the workspace: map evidence → Keep a few → **Generate** → download the CV.
4. Sign out / sign in as the seeded user → you go straight to `/roleproof` (established), never the wall.

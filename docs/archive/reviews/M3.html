✨ The one-line

The coach went from a scrollable stack of cards to a calm rail + one focused question. Answer → AI drafts → approve (or just hit Enter), and the next-best question loads itself. There's an 8-second Undo that truly reverts the graph, and after a few captures a designed stop — because "never done" lives in the meter, not a nagging todo count.

✅ What I changed

✓
Rail + stage. Strength (with the M1 breakdown bars), a live session tally, and "Up next" on the left; one question at a time on the right.
✓
Auto-advance. Approve/skip → the next-highest-value prompt loads itself. Enter approves.
✓
Real undo. A toast and per-item undo in the tally — both fully reverse the graph edit (delete a created story/metric, or restore prior values), and remove any follow-up it spawned. Verified by a round-trip test (strength returned to exactly its prior value).
✓
Bounded sessions. "Today's set · N of 3" and a designed stop card ("Nice set — 3 captured, +N strength") with a "keep going" option.
✓
Responsive. Two-pane on wide screens; the rail stacks above the stage on mobile.

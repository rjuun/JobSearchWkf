The old meter saturated at 100 and went dead. The new one is five honest dimensions, and the biggest slice — Relevancy to the roles you're chasing — only fills as you prove those roles' requirements. ✅ What I changed

✓
Five-dimension model. Foundation, Quantified impact, ATS coverage, Freshness, and Relevancy-to-targets — replacing the flat formula that maxed out on any decent profile.
✓
Never saturates. Relevancy follows a curve that approaches but never reaches its cap, and freshness decays over time — so there's always somewhere to grow.
✓
Flag a target = new headroom. A ★ toggle on each role. Flagging raises the ceiling (75 → 100) and pulls that role's requirements into the coach — it never drops your score.
✓
Breakdown on the profile. The strength hero now shows all five bars, the headroom, and a "flag a target to unlock" nudge when relevancy is locked.
✓
7 new tests (33 total) — coverage grows, never-saturation, flag-raises-ceiling, no-drop-on-flag, freshness decay.

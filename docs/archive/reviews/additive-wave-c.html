The differentiation wave, still additive · ✓ Applied ⚑ flag-gated

✨ The one-line

Four genuinely new capabilities land beside the shipped app — a Story through-line generated from your evidence, a Discover surface that mirrors who you are and opens adjacent doors, an Excavation coach session that digs up buried history, and an opt-in public Proof Link. Each reuses existing machinery (the LLM choke point, the B6 scorer, the coach queue, the strength meter) rather than inventing new pipelines.

✅ What shipped (Wave C)

C1
"Your story" — the through-line. One LLM pass over your approved evidence (via the same runStructured choke point) writes a career arc plus copy-out cover-letter and LinkedIn drafts; versions are append-only. Quietly pilots the cover-letter step the manual process had but the app never did. Nothing invented — same anti-fabrication guard as the CV steps.
C2
Mirror + Unexpected Doors. A curated set of role archetypes scored against your graph with the existing B6 requirement-alignment formula (same maths, new inputs). Mirror reflects who your evidence says you are; doors are adjacent families you score well on. A door's "flag as target" reuses B3 — it materialises a real target lead with its requirements.
C3
Excavation sessions. A fifth coach engine (engine5Prompts) seeds era-targeted invitations from thinly-documented roles. Lowest value in the system, so they never queue ahead of real work — an optional "dig here" card (⛏), gated by NEXT_EXCAVATION.
C4
Proof Link. An opt-in, per-user public page at /p/<token> (default OFF) showing a read-only proof summary — strength, evidence depth, targets — and no contact details or raw evidence. Ships last: it's the only surface that leaves the authenticated app.

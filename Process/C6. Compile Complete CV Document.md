### Purpose  

Compile all outputs from previous steps (C2, C3, C4, and C5) into a clean, professional, final CV tailored to a specific Job Lead. The final CV must remain within **2 pages maximum** and follow strict space management and prioritization rules to ensure conciseness and relevance.

---

### A. Inputs

- Final cleaned **C2 export** for the Job Lead (particularly the “CV Position” column)
- CV Bullets produced in [[C3. Transform Evidence into CV Bullets]] 
- Profile text drafted in [[C5. Drafting CV Profile (Per Job Lead)]]
- Job Lead details (Job Title, City/Office, reference codes if available)
- Working  [[CV_Template.docx]]

---

### B. Execution Sequence

1. **Load the Working Template**  
   Start with the current approved CV Template.

2. **Apply Space Management & Prioritization Rules**
   - Profile: Maximum **5 lines**
   - Skills section: Maximum **4 categories**, with a maximum of **5 skills per category**
   - Only include CV Bullets linked to **Core or Important** skills from the Job Lead
   - Project labels remain visible in the final CV

3. **Replace Placeholders**
   - Use the **"CV Position"** column from the final C2 export to map each CV Bullet to its correct location in the template.
   - Completely replace all `<< >>` placeholders with the actual CV Bullets.
   - No `<< >>` placeholders should remain visible in the final CV.

4. **Compile Each Section**

| Section                     | Source                          | Rules |
|----------------------------|----------------------------------|-------|
| **Header + Job Title**     | Job Lead details                | Include Job Title and reference code (if available) |
| **Personal Info**          | Template + Job Lead City        | Add “(Willing to relocate to [City])” if the Job Lead office is not Vienna |
| **Profile**                | C5                              | Maximum 5 lines. Apply bold inline only when natural |
| **Skills**                 | Filtered from C2/C4             | Max 4 categories. Prioritize Core → Important skills |
| **Professional Experience**| C3 + C4 bullets                 | Map using "CV Position". Project labels remain. Only show bullets linked to Core or Important skills |
| **Education**              | Fixed                           | Keep shortened version |
| **Executive Education**    | Fixed                           | Keep as-is |
| **Languages**              | Fixed                           | Small separate section at the bottom |

5. **Headshot Handling**
   - If a headshot is to be included: Insert according to rules defined in C1.
   - If no headshot is included: Insert the following text:  
     *"Headshot not added in respect to D&I best practices."*

6. **Final Quality Check**
   - Confirm that no `<< >>` placeholders remain.
   - Verify the CV does not exceed **2 pages**.
   - Check that all Core and Important skills are represented at least once.
   - Ensure consistent formatting and professional appearance.

7. **Output**
   - Final clean CV in Word format (PDF optional).
   - File naming should follow the convention defined in C1 (if applicable).

---

### C. Space Management & Prioritization Rules

| Rule                           | Limit                                      | Notes |
|--------------------------------|--------------------------------------------|-------|
| **Maximum Pages**              | Strictly **2 pages**                       | Non-negotiable |
| **Profile**                    | Maximum **5 lines**                        | Keep concise and relevant to the Job Lead |
| **Skills Section**             | Max **4 categories**, max **5 skills per category** | Prioritize Core → Important skills |
| **Bullet Inclusion**           | Only include bullets linked to Core or Important skills | Project labels remain visible |
| **Role Overview Label**        | Keep **"Role Overview:"**                  | As per current decision |
| **Languages**                  | Small separate section at the bottom       | Fixed content |

---

### D. Notes & Quality Rules

- The final CV must look like a **professional, finished document**, not a working template.
- All placeholders (`<< >>`) must be fully replaced before output.
- The CV should feel focused and relevant to the specific Job Lead.
- Education and Executive Education remain fixed and shortened.
- Languages remain as a small, separate section at the bottom.
- This step is the final compilation before the CV is ready for submission.
- Project labels remain visible in the final CV (as per latest decision).

## 1. What is the problem or opportunity?

Improve Screening by introducing an Environment Gate Check

Observation: Two of the last three leads (Philips, Deloitte) gate on an environment the candidate doesn't have — corporate-M&A and professional-services/consulting respectively. Both score in the 6.4–6.8 band, dragged down by a single structural Core requirement rather than by overall capability.  
Suggested action: Consider adding an early B-phase "environment gate" check — flag leads whose Core qualifications require a career environment (consulting, Big-4, startup, specific industry) the profile lacks, before investing B5/B6 time. This would let the in-house-transformation strengths be aimed at in-house transformation roles, where the same evidence scores far higher.  
Where it applies: B2 (Identify Roadblocks) or B3 (Identify Misalignments) — pre-scoring triage.
